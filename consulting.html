PRINCIPLES
  1. Outcomes over Outputs. I anchor every project on the business outcome it must unlock—growth, margin, or mission—then work backward to the smallest shippable step that moves the metric. This keeps teams focused on what matters and gives stakeholders early proof that we’re on the right path.
  2. Momentum through Clarity. Ambiguity stalls progress, so I invest in ruthless prioritization and crisp, shared definitions of success. When everyone knows why and what’s next, velocity rises, bottlenecks surface fast, and energy stays high.
  3. Build with, not for. Whether I’m guiding a product squad or advising a C-suite, I treat people as partners, not passengers. I ask first, listen long, and co-create solutions—because change that’s written with the team is the change that sticks.
  1. Evidence-Powered Storytelling. I pair rigorous analytics with human narrative: numbers reveal the signal, stories move hearts and budgets. This blend wins alignment at the whiteboard and confidence in the boardroom.
  2. Stewardship from Vision to Value. Strategy excites me, but stewardship defines me. I stay engaged from the first hypothesis through launch, iteration, and ROI, adjusting course as markets shift and keeping the long game in view.
