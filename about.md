## How I Got Here

As an engineer at Rheonics, I transformed a research-grade in-line viscosity sensor into a commercial product now used in more than 15 industry verticals. Starting from zero, I knit together hardware, firmware, software, supply-chain partners, and a go-to-market plan. Along the way I built the company’s core infrastructure and workflows — leading marketing, sales, quality, and certification functions.

When AI began reshaping industry, I joined Bosch AIShield to help define — and launch — the still-nascent field of AI security. I shaped the product story, landed the venture’s first enterprise champions, and took the conversation to public-policy forums.

Before starting my MBA, I embedded with Bosch’s software division to see strategy at its largest scale. There I helped steer executive focus toward domain-led platforms, aligning the portfolio, GTM, and M&A pipeline around bold deep-tech bets.

I learned a few things along the way. 

- AI firepower paired with judgement, trust, and connection is where most leverage lives.
- Effecting change is hard and incremental momentum is the key to success.
- Managing and hiring people is rooted in unlocking potential, leading with clarity, empathy and earned influence, not judging performance.
